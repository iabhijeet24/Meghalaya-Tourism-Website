TOURS & PACKAGES

Explore the rich history, vibrant culture, and welcoming people of this scenic Indian state.

Shillong City Tour

Enjoy the legendary sights of Sohra and explore the cultural and natural landmarks of Jaintia Hills. Walk under the shadows of an ancient canopy in the Sacred Grove in Mawphlang, feel the breeze on the edge of the immense Laitlum canyon and marvel at the rock formations and pristine nature of Mawsynram.

Cherrapunji Adventure

Hit all the top spots in the Khasi Hills with this tour package. Wander around in one of Asia’s most endearing natural golf courses, hit the mountain highways and view splendorous waterfalls and marvel at a living root bridge, a feat of natural engineering unique to the communities of southern Meghalaya.

Dawki & Mawlynnong

This itinerary packages some of the best landmarks and destinations in the state of Meghalaya. From the natural and cultural attractions of Sohra to the breathtaking landscape of Laitlum Canyon, interspersed with the vibrancy of Shillong – you will experience layers of life in the Khasi Hills. Download for more information.

Meghalaya Cultural Trail

The verdant valleys of the southern ranges, a meandering river, the riveting cliffs of Sohra and the charm of Shillong – these are some of the memorable experiences in store for you with this comprehensive 6-day itinerary. Another highlight is the trek to the double-decker Living Root Bridge of Nongriat. Download for more details.

Adventure

Discover thrilling activities like caving, hikes & treks, paddling, and more.

Caving

Explore a subterranean labyrinth and discover what lies beneath. Meghalaya’s caves are legendary and this tour takes you through some of the most stunning limestone passages and formations.

Hikes & Treks

Shnongpdeng, a village by the Umngot river is famed as a site for water-based and land-based adventures. Some of these include ziplining, scuba diving and more. The best way to experience these is by signing up for a short trip with one of the best adventure teams in Meghalaya.

Trekking-Mawsynram

Storm through mountain and forest trails – hit some of the most scenic highways in the country and enjoy detours that will introduce you to amazing new cultures and practices. Meghalaya on a bike is a dream for all adventurers, guaranteed to be a story you will never forget.

River Canoeing

Rapelling down the face of a waterfall, wading through fresh pools and soaking in the most pristine and undisturbed spots in the hills of Meghalaya – river canyoning and waterfall rappelling are some of the most unique experiences you will have anywhere.

Rural Bike Riding

Hit the highways of Meghalaya on a motorbike and feel the wind in your face. And your pit stops will include serene campsites and local homestays for authentic experiences. There are very few words that can do justice to the thrill and excitement that motorcyclist adventurers have when exploring new places.
